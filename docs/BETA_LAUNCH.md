# Beta Launch Plan

## Audience

- 20-30 musicians across cover creators, livestream artists, and worship teams.
- Device spread: laptop keyboard, MIDI controllers, and foot pedal users.

## Launch Sequence

1. Week 1: Closed invite cohort with onboarding call.
2. Week 2: Usage monitoring and weekly office hour.
3. Week 3: Export quality feedback sprint and reliability fixes.
4. Week 4: Pricing test with Pro checkout conversion flow.

## Metrics

- Activation: first successful import and playback in first session.
- Value: number of exported takes per active user.
- Reliability: stuck-note and crash reports per 100 sessions.
- Revenue signal: checkout attempts and completed subscription events.

## Feedback Channels

- In-app issue report form (next iteration).
- Weekly feedback form tied to session logs.
- Priority escalation for playback and recording regressions.
